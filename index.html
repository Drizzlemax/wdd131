Harare, Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe is a landlocked country in southern Africa known for its diverse landscapes and wildlife. Its capital, Harare, is a bustling city full of culture and history. Zimbabwe is home to Victoria Falls, one of the Seven Natural Wonders of the World, as well as diverse flora and fauna. It has a rich history, with ancient stone cities like Great Zimbabwe as part of its heritage.
