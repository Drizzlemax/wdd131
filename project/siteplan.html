Hiking Routes in Zimbabwe - Site Plan

Site Name

Zimbabwe Hiking Trails

This name clearly represents the focus of the website, which is to provide information about hiking trails across Zimbabwe. The name is simple, direct, and easily identifiable, ensuring that visitors will immediately understand the website's purpose.

Domain name: zimhikingtrails.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to serve as a comprehensive guide for hikers in Zimbabwe. It will provide details on various trails, their difficulty levels, locations, distances, and best seasons to hike. Additionally, the site will offer safety tips, essential gear recommendations, and the ability for users to share their experiences.

Scenarios

  • Which hiking trail in Zimbabwe is best for a beginner?
  • Where can I find information on hiking safety in the Matopos National Park?
